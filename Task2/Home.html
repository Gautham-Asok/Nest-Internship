About Kerala Government

The Government of Kerala works for the welfare and development of the people through various departments such as health, education, transport, labour, local self-government, and public administration. Kerala is known for its high literacy rate, healthcare system, and social development. The ministers and government departments work together to improve the quality of life of citizens across the state.
